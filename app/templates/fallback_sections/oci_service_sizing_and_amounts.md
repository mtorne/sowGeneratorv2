OCI service sizing and quantities will be determined during the architectural design phase and validated against the client's workload performance and availability requirements.

Initial estimates are based on current infrastructure capacity metrics, expected traffic patterns, and target SLAs agreed upon during pre-engagement discovery. Compute shapes, storage tiers, and network configurations will be right-sized to balance cost efficiency with operational resilience.

Final service amounts are subject to revision following load testing and Oracle's workload assessment recommendations. A detailed BOM (Bill of Materials) will be delivered as an addendum to this Statement of Work prior to provisioning.
